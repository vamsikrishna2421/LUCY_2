The Pitch

LUCY in 60 seconds

You said it once. Three days later, your phone remembered it for you.

LUCY runs in the background of your life, capturing the stream of thoughts you would normally lose — a task, a person, an expense, an idea — and silently organizes everything into a private, encrypted second brain you can query like a conversation. No folders, no tags, no setup. Speak or type one messy sentence; LUCY does the filing.

The problem

Your best thoughts arrive at the worst times — walking, driving, mid-meeting. Notes apps demand structure you do not have a free hand for, so the thought is lost. Most "AI memory" tools send everything to the cloud and hallucinate confident nonsense.

The solution

One capture box. LUCY extracts tasks, people, expenses, reminders, ideas and follow-ups from plain language, splits multi-event journals into separate memories, and connects them into a knowledge graph with evidence and confidence — never invented certainty.

Why it is different

Local-first and encrypted (SQLCipher on-device). Bring your own model — Claude or OpenAI. It does not just store; it acts: one tap to call, navigate, set a timer, add a reminder, or schedule an event. And it surfaces what matters proactively.

The 90-second demo

  1. Open LUCY. Speak one sentence: "Had a great call with Marcus about the Series B deck — he wants the revenue slide revised by Thursday. Also pick up wine for dinner Friday, about 40 dollars."
  2. Watch it become structured memory in seconds: a task (revise revenue slide, due Thursday), a follow-up on Marcus, an expense ($40 wine, Friday) — from one breath.
  3. Ask: "What do I need to do before Thursday?" — LUCY answers from your own memory, with sources.
  4. Paste a whole day journal → it fans out into separate timeline memories, one headline per event.
  5. Say "Call Marcus" → a confirmation card slides up → one tap → the dialer opens.

Privacy

Your data stays
on your phone.

Four layers of privacy architecture, designed from day one. No compromises.

1
Local-first storage
All data stored in SQLite on your device. Never transmitted without your action. Works fully offline. No account, no login, no tracking.
2
Privacy classification per capture
You can mark any capture as "Contains private details" before sending. LUCY's extraction also detects sensitive patterns automatically. Three levels: normal / local / private.
3
On-device masking before remote AI
When remote intelligence is enabled and a private thought needs AI processing, the on-device model replaces sensitive content with placeholders first. "[PERSON_1] mentioned [FINANCIAL_DETAIL_1]" — never real names or numbers.
4
Selective and auditable remote
Remote intelligence is entirely optional and off by default. Only the specific text you ask about is sent — never raw device data, camera, location, or battery readings. Your OpenAI key is stored in the device's secure keychain.
