# LUCY — Hackathon Product Review
*Reviewer: Senior Product Designer (ex-Anthropic, ex-OpenAI)*

## Core Verdict
Strong thesis, muddled execution in the demo surface. The bones are right.

## The Killer Demo Sequence (90 seconds)
1. Open app → orbital splash plays (10s)
2. Mic button → speak: "Had a great call with Marcus about the Series B deck. He wants the revenue slide revised by Thursday. Also pick up wine for dinner Friday, like $40." (20s)
3. Watch: task card appears (revise slide), expense extracted ($40), follow-up on Marcus logged — all from one sentence (15s)
4. Ask: "What do I need to do before Thursday?" → LUCY responds with context (20s)
5. Ask: "Call Marcus" → automation card slides up → one tap → iOS dialer (15s)

## Top 5 User-Confusing Moments
1. **Empty board** — "Your board is clear" kills demo. No judge waits for data to accumulate.
2. **5 sub-tabs in Today** — abstract labels, paralysis
3. **Meeting Mode unreachable** — hidden behind scroll/header
4. **"Contains private details" checkbox** — anxiety-inducing, technical copy
5. **Onboarding shows no output** — text only, no before/after visual

## Cut These (dilute vision)
- Library sub-tabs: Interests, Places
- Memory tab's raw knowledge graph view
- "View structure" / "STRUCTURED MEMORY" toggle
- "AI Actions" menu on individual captures

## The Missing Wow Feature
**Live Capture Replay** — after processing a capture, animate the extracted items appearing one by one for 4-5 seconds (task card drops in, person lights up, expense chip appears). Makes the invisible visible. Buildable in a day.

## The Pitch (3 sentences)
*You said it once. Three days later your phone remembered it for you. LUCY runs in the background of your phone, listening to the stream of thoughts you'd normally lose, and silently organizes everything into a personal knowledge graph you can query like a conversation.*

## Priority Actions (in order)
1. Seed demo data on first launch (2-3 hrs) ← most critical
2. Live Capture Replay animation (4-6 hrs) ← the wow moment
3. Fix "Contains private details" copy (1-2 hrs)
4. Collapse Today to 3 tabs (2-3 hrs)
5. Make Meeting Mode discoverable (30 min)

## One Bright Spot
The orbital splash animation is genuinely beautiful — one of the best custom splash screens in any hackathon submission. Don't let judges tap through it too fast.
