Getting Started

How to use LUCY

No setup required. Just start capturing and LUCY does the rest.

1
Capture a thought
Type anything naturally in the Board screen input. No formatting needed. LUCY understands English, Telugu, Tanglish, Hindi, and mixed languages.
"Had a meeting with Sam about Q3 budget. He'll send the spreadsheet by Friday. Need to follow up with design team."
2
LUCY organizes it automatically
In seconds, tasks, people, follow-ups, and key info are extracted. Tap "View structure" on any memory in Today → Captured to see what she understood.
3
Enable passive listening during meetings
Tap the Listen button in the header. LUCY transcribes everything in the room every 10 minutes. A red indicator confirms recording is active.
4
Ask questions about your day
Open the Ask tab and ask anything. For richer answers, go to Settings → Remote Intelligence and add your OpenAI API key.
"What did I work on today?" · "What tasks do I have?" · "Summarize my progress this week"
